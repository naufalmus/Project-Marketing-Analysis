# 📊 Customer Segmentation for Targeted Marketing Strategy

## 📌 Overview
This project aims to segment customers based on their purchasing behavior in order to support more effective and targeted marketing strategies. By leveraging data analysis and clustering techniques, the project identifies distinct customer groups that can be approached differently to maximize business value.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
The company currently applies a general marketing strategy to all customers without considering their differences in behavior and value. This approach can lead to:
- Ineffective marketing campaigns  
- Wasted promotional budget  
- Low customer retention  

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Segment customers using clustering techniques  
- Identify key characteristics of each customer group  
- Provide actionable business recommendations for each segment  

---

## ⚙️ Methodology
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  
- Feature Selection (Income, Total Spending, Recency)  
- Data Scaling  
- K-Means Clustering  
- Elbow Method for optimal cluster selection  
- Cluster evaluation and interpretation  

---

## 📊 Key Features Used
- **Income** → Customer purchasing power  
- **Total Spending** → Customer value  
- **Recency** → Customer activity level  

---

## 🔍 Results
The analysis identified **3 main customer segments**:

1. **High Value Active Customers**  
   - Recently active  
   - High spending  
   - High income  

2. **Mid Value Customers**  
   - متوسط activity and spending  
   - Potential for growth  

3. **Churn Risk Customers**  
   - Long time since last purchase  
   - Low spending  

---

## 💡 Business Insights
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to revenue  
- Some customers show high potential but need engagement  
- A segment of customers is at risk of churn and requires reactivation  

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

### High Value Customers
- Loyalty programs  
- Exclusive offers  
- Premium product targeting  

### Mid Value Customers
- Upselling & cross-selling  
- Personalized promotions  

### Churn Risk Customers
- Discount campaigns  
- Reactivation strategies  
- Retargeting ads  

---

## 📈 Expected Impact
- More effective marketing campaigns  
- Improved customer retention  
- Better allocation of marketing budget  
- Increased overall revenue
